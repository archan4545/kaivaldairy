Our Story

Pure Taste.
Pure Tradition.

At Kaival Dairy, we believe in the power of pure, fresh, and traditional dairy. From our humble beginnings as Shreeji Dairy to becoming Kaival — we've served Himatnagar's families with love, quality, and consistency for over 15 years.

Every product is crafted with care using 100% pure milk — no shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromises. Our Mango Ras is freshly prepared daily, and our Basundi collection features 12+ unique flavours that you won't find anywhere else.
